Hello, I am Sinéad.

There was a time where I felt like I hadn’t control over my eating habits and my body I knew I was carrying a bit of extra weight but these extra pounds were having a detrimental effect on my mindset about my body my abilities and my self-esteem.

I tried different types of diets but never succeeded why? My mindset, a fear, frustration and the over complication of what it would take to regain control and get back in to shape Now don’t get me wrong losing weight is not easy but once I changed my mindset about training and nutrition a lot fell in to place.

I started to eat well not to lose weight but to feel better I started to think of training not as a punishment and instead fell in love with the fact movement made me happy. I enjoyed lifting weights and getting stronger this made me realise the gym was not just all treadmills and lettuce leaves but a place of many different people, all there with the want to improve.

I remember starting my journey alone, no accountability and honestly a lack of knowledge with all these ideas what including a training lifestyle “was” and not actually is. As I got older my passion for movement continued, eventually working my way from someone who had a fear of the gym to someone who coaches, educates and simplifies the fitness process for a living!

Weather that want is muscle gain, fat loss, or purely the fact that you want to feel better within yourself I’m happy to say I can help you.

You have to trust the process, commit and realise there is no perfect time to start the time is now!
